Message from the Desk

Dear Stake Holders,
Warm Greetings of the Day...!

Indeed, I am very much delighted to extend warm welcome to you all to the Government Polytechnic College in the Ziro valley of Lower Subansiri District, Arunachal Pradesh. It is a Diploma level Technical Education institute and offers three years full time regular Diploma Engineering Programs in the Civil Engineering (CE), Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE). The start of the academic activities at GPC Ziro from the session Jul-Dec 2025 marks a new and prosperous beginning in the field of Technical Education in the state. It would immensely benefit the aspiring budding technocrats of the state as Technical Education is now more accessible.

I feel privileged to lead the institute as the founder Principal (I/c); towards achieving excellence in Technical Education aiming to develop technocrats with sound technical knowledge equipped with industry-oriented computer/software skills.

In fact, we have just started the academic activities and have numerous challenges ahead. We are confident that all the challenges will be meticulously managed and overcome as we march ahead with the support of Hon’ble MLA, District Adminsitration, Higher Authorities and other stake holders.

It is our endeavor to nurture the students academically, technically and also professionally. We, as a Team GPC Ziro, with the experienced, sincere and dedicated faculty and staff members, leave no stone unturned to impart quality Technical Education.

We aim to make the GPC Ziro as the one of the best institutes offering Diploma Technical Education in the state/region. We invite the stake holders to share their suggestion and feedback on improving quality of Technical Education. The Team GPC Ziro encourages all the students to make the best use of their precious time at GPCZ engaging themselves in teaching and learning process while participating in extra-curricular and technical activities thereby contributing to the overall growth of the institute.

I take this opportunity to extend my best wishes to all the stake holders of the GPC Ziro and hope to march together on the path of achieving excellence in Technical Education.

The journey has just begun… We have miles to go … Lots to achieve.
Jai Hind * Jai Arunachal

Respectfully,

Dr. D. Devarasiddappa
Principal I/c
