Our Vision

To Become an Institute of Academic Excellence in Diploma Technical Education harnessing the overall development of the Budding Technocrats of the state with Professionalism, entrepreneurial and industry-oriented skills; fostering Sustainable Socio-Economic Growth of the Region.

Our Mission

  • Impart quality Diploma Technical Education with hands-on-practice building technical competence and problem-solving skills.
  • Collaborate with Industries/Organizations/Institutions to promote entrepreneurial activities, industrial training & interactions and mutual exchange of faculty & staff.
  • Inculcate professionalism with discipline, lifelong learning & creativity amongst the faculties, staff and students.
  • Conduct tailored skill-oriented training programs to enhance employability of students and local youth thereby promoting socio-economic progress.

Core Values

Integrity and Ethics

Upholding honesty, transparency, and responsibility in all academic and professional activities.

Excellence in Learning

Commitment to quality technical education, continuous improvement, and imparting practical skills.

Innovation and Creativity

Encouraging curiosity, problem-solving, and innovative thinking to address real-world challenges.

Inclusiveness and Equity

Promoting equal opportunities for students from diverse socio-economic and tribal backgrounds.

Respect and Discipline

Fostering a culture of mutual respect, professionalism, and disciplined work habits.

Social Responsibility

Serving society through sustainable engineering practices and community-oriented initiatives.

Collaboration and Teamwork

Building strong partnerships with industry, academia, and government, while nurturing teamwork among students and staff.

Accountability and Responsibility

Being accountable for all the actions and deliver the duty with greater responsivity in the larger interest of the institute.

Peer Appreciation and Cooperation

Inculcating the habit of peer appreciation and mutual cooperation amongst the faculty/staff members in curricular/co-curricular activities and research work.
