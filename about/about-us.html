Institute Profile

Government Polytechnic College Ziro is a Diploma Level Technical Institution situated in the picturesque Ziro Valley in Lower Subansiri District of Arunachal Pradesh, India. Established in the year 2025 under the Directorate of Higher & Technical Education, Itanagar, the Institute is dedicated for imparting quality Diploma Technical Education in the state. The institute offers three years Diploma Engineering Programs in Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering and Electrical & Electronics Engineering; preparing students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

The polytechnic college was established under the scheme "Sub-Mission on Polytechnics" of Govt. of India; aimed at setting up of polytechnics in the uncovered districts across the nation. The primary goal of the scheme was to expand the reach of Technical Education and skill development, especially in rural and disadvantaged areas, to enable local communities to obtain gainful employment. The scheme provided access to Technical Education, specially to aspirational districts for skill development.

The institute is located in the picturesque Ziro valley in the Lower Subansiri District of Indian state Arunachal Pradesh. It is located at Village: Jugo, Tajang, PO: Ziro-791120, which is 6 kms from the Old Ziro market and 2 kms from the Siikhe Lake-a tourist attraction of the Ziro valley. Being away from disturbances of main city and located in a peaceful environment, GPC Ziro is an ideal place for learning with nature.
