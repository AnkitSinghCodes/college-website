Summer Internships

AICTE has made 7-10 weeks summer internships mandatory in the new curriculum which will equip the students with practical understanding and training about industry practices in a suitable industry or organization. Accordingly, all the students have to undergone mandatory internship as per the curriculum in two phases.

Summer Internship-I

In the first phase, all the students have to attend Summer Internship-I (SI201) of 3-4 weeks duration after the completion of 2nd semester i.e. in the month of May-June. After successful completion of the Summer Internship-I, the students will earn 2 (two) credits. Summer Internship-I should be undertaken in an Industry/Govt. or Pvt. Certified Agencies which are in social sector/ Govt. Skill Centers/Institutes/Schemes.

Summer Internship-II

In the second phase, the students shall attend the SI301: Summer Internship-II of 5-6 weeks duration after the completion of 4th semester i.e. in the month of May-June. Every student will earn 3 credits after successful completion of the Summer Internship-II. As per AICTE guidelines, the SI301 should only be undertaken in a relevant industry.

The students will have to attend the SI 201 and SI301 mandatorily and maintain daily diary in the prescribed format and submit it to the Training and Placement in-charge within the due date specified.

Evaluation of the SI 201 and SI301 will be done during the End Semester examination and is based on work done, quality of report, performance in viva-voce, presentation.

The Training and Placement In-charge will coordinate the Summer Internship-I and II and students have to follow the instructions/guidelines issued time to time.
