Training & Placement Cell

The Training & Placement Cell of the GPC, Ziro primarily aims to arrange the mandatory summer internship training programs as per the curriculum requirement and assist the students getting job in public and private sector companies through on campus and off campus interviews.

The TPO cell also aims to organize/conduct soft & hard skill training programs, mock interviews and entrepreneurship training programs to enhance the employability of the students.
