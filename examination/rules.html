General Examination Rules

  • Students must carry their Admit Card and College ID card to the examination hall.
  • Mobile phones, smartwatches, and programmable calculators are strictly prohibited inside the examination hall.
  • Students should report to the examination center at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • No student will be allowed to leave the examination hall before completion of one hour of the exam.

Attendance Requirements

  • A minimum of 75% attendance is mandatory to act for the End Semester Examination.
  • Condonation of shortage of attendance up to 15% may be granted by the Principal on medical grounds.

Malpractice & Unfair Means

Any student found guilty of malpractice or using unfair means during the examination shall be liable for punishment as per the institute's rules, which may include cancellation of the examination or debarment from future exams.
