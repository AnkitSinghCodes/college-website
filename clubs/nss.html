About NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at Government Polytechnic College Ziro is dedicated to developing students' personality through community service. NSS volunteers work on various social welfare activities, contributing to nation-building while developing leadership and organizational skills.

Our NSS unit actively participates in cleanliness drives, blood donation camps, awareness campaigns, and rural development programs. Through these activities, students learn the value of social responsibility and develop a deeper connection with the community.

Our Objectives

Community Service

Engage students in meaningful community service activities to develop social awareness and responsibility.

Leadership Development

Foster leadership qualities and organizational skills through various NSS programs and initiatives.

Environmental Awareness

Promote environmental consciousness and sustainable living practices through environmental conservation.
