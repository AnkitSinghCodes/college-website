Building Tomorrow's Engineers

Government Polytechnic College Ziro is a Diploma Level Technical Institution situated in the picturesque Ziro Valley in Lower Subansiri District of Arunachal Pradesh, India. Established in the year 2025 under the Directorate of Higher & Technical Education, Itanagar, the Institute is dedicated for imparting quality Diploma Technical Education in the state.

The institute offers three years Diploma Engineering Programs in Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering and Electrical & Electronics Engineering; preparing students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Learn More

Our Vision

To Become an Institute of Academic Excellence in Diploma Technical Education harnessing the overall development of the Budding Technocrats of the state with Professionalism, entrepreneurial and industry-oriented skills; fostering Sustainable Socio-Economic Growth of the Region.

Our Mission

  • Impart quality Diploma Technical Education with hands-on-practice building technical competence and problem-solving skills.
  • Collaborate with Industries/Institutions to promote entrepreneurial activities and industrial training.
  • Inculcate professionalism with discipline, lifelong learning & creativity.
  • Conduct tailored skill-oriented training programs to enhance employability of students.
