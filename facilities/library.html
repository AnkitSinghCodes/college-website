ABOUT THE COLLEGE LIBRARY

The library of the instittue was established the year 2025 to cater the academic needs and requirements of the students, faculty and non-teaching staff. It has adequate space with all basic infrastructure facility. The library has all necessary books and learning materials needed to enrich the knowledge and skills of the students/learners.

The library has a collection of adequate number of printed books catering to the needs of students and fauclty memebers. The book shelves are organized department-wise and books are arranged by subject.

At present, the library has a separate reading arena equiped with dedicated reading tables having a seating capacity of 20-30 persons. Following an open access system, students can borrow up to 4 books for 15 days, while faculty and other staff can borrow up to 5 books for 30 days.

The library shares the computers of the Langauage Laboratory with 24/7 internet connectivity and is accessible to the students as per their convenience/demand.

Library induction program is carried out every year for new students in which details about the library collection, services and timing are briefed. The queries of the students regarding the use of learning resoucers of the library are also attended during the program.

In the coming years, the library of the institute aims to have vast collection of wide range of books for the all the Diploma Engineering Programs offered. Also, plans to provide the students with online books and other library services as deemed necessary time to time with the use of technology.
