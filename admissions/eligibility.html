Admission to Government Polytechnic College Ziro is governed by the rules and regulations laid down by the Directorate of Higher & Technical Education (DHTE), Government of Arunachal Pradesh.

Educational Qualification

For All Diploma Courses

Candidates must have passed Class X (High School Leaving Certificate) or equivalent examination from a recognized board.

  • Minimum 35% marks in aggregate.
  • Must have Mathematics and Science as compulsory subjects.

Residential Requirement

Domicile

Preference is given to candidates with Arunachal Pradesh Scheduled Tribe (APST) certificate. Non-APST candidates may apply under the open quota subject to availability of seats and DHTE norms.

Note: Eligibility criteria are subject to change as per the latest notifications from DHTE. Please verify with the official brochure.
