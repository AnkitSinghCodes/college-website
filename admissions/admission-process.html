Admission to Government Polytechnic College Ziro is governed by the rules and regulations laid down by the Arunachal Pradesh State Council for Technical Education (APSCTE) under the Directorate of Higher & Technical Education (DHTE), Government of Arunachal Pradesh.

01

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the minimum academic requirements for the diploma course you wish to apply for.

02

Fill Online Application

Visit the DHTE admission portal and fill out the online application form with personal and academic details.

03

Entrance Exam (APJEE) / Merit Selection

Conduct of APJEE (Diploma) is kept in abeyance w.e.f. the Academic Year 2026-27 till further orders vide Govt. Notification No. HTE-18/1/2023-HTE-BR-HTE dated 11-03-2026.

Selection and nomination of eligible candidates for admission in the various DIPLOMA programmes shall be done on the basis of merit in the qualifying examination i.e. Class X or Class XII as the case may be.

04

Counselling & Seat Allotment

Qualified candidates must attend the counselling session for seat allotment based on their rank and preference.

05

Document Verification & Admission

Report to the college with original documents for verification and pay the admission fees to confirm your seat.
